Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to everything you need to know about our resume builder and AI analyzer.

Is Pix Resume completely free?

Yes! You can build, analyze, and export your standard resume to PDF without spending a dime. We also offer Premium services with advanced AI cover letters and unlimited resume tracking.

Is my data safe with your AI?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption for all uploaded documents. Your PDFs are immediately deleted from our processing servers after the analysis is complete.

Does it work for non-tech roles?

Yes, our AI is trained on hundreds of thousands of job descriptions across all industries including finance, healthcare, marketing, and more. It evaluates keywords effectively across any domain.

Are the templates ATS-friendly?

100%. Our templates are strictly coded without internal complex tables or images that trip up Applicant Tracking Systems, ensuring your resume gets read by human eyes.
